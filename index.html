About

Greetings! I am a junior at the University of Arizona, majoring in Software Engineering with a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology. My academic journey has been enriched by hands-on experiences and exciting opportunities. During my college journey, I had the incredible opportunity to intern at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory. Working alongside brilliant minds in a real-world setting, I contributed to projects that fueled my enthusiasm for software engineering and its applications in space exploration. Beyond my internship, I am actively engaged in serving on the student advisory board for the software engineering department, as well as participating as a member of the Software Engineering Wildcats club.

Looking ahead, my vision is to seamlessly transition from academia to industry upon obtaining my Bachelors of Science in Software Engineering. My journey so far has been marked by transformative experiences, including a year-long internship at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, a research position at the University of Arizona, and an internship at Garmin Inc. These diverse roles have not only expanded my technical skills but have also fueled my passion for real-world problem-solving. I look forward to continuing to grow my career and my knowledge in industry.
